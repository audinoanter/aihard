Our processes

1.

Strategy workshop

First, we need to understand your company better. We organize a 3-day onsite workshop at your office to find the most effective improvements that would provide the highest RoI in the shortest time.

2.

Data Collection and Exploration

Our team will find, capture, analyze, clean, and prepare required datasets. Then, we will perform an exploratory analysis phase to find patterns and correlations. If necessary, we will buy or collect external data for you.

3.

Model Development

Our experts will execute thousands of experiments in parallel to develop a machine learning model - a core of a machine learning system - trained on historical data. It can predict the future trends or understand the semantics of a text.

4.

Full-stack application development

We develop all necessary features to access the model in a user-friendly way. Scalable and with state-of-the-art security. Integrate the model with a REST API or a front-end application.
